Our History

I developed BinText Converter post my successful completion of Harvard University's CS50x: Introduction to Computer Science. Drawing inspiration from the course's "Bulbs" problem set, this web app turns text messages into captivating binary bulb-like patterns, delivering an engaging user experience.

How It Works

  1. Input the text or binary data of your choice into the designated textarea.
  2. The Flask backend system will then individually convert each character of your provided message into its corresponding Unicode value.
  3. You will receive output.
